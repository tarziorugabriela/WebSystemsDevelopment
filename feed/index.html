Now is the time to know the masters behind the scene…


The BACA team consists of 3 students(Amihaesei Stefan, Ionita Delia, Tarzioru Stefanica) from the Faculty of Economics and Business Administration who are passionate about technology and travel.

Often they have been lost when they go on hikes, having to use many applications, one for the itinerary, another for the weather, another to book their flight. They noted that there is no application on the market that have all this features together. And as a result of this discovered need, BACA web project appeared.
